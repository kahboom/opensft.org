What we do

We believe that fashion needs open source now more than ever, but we can't do it without your help. We're here to help you unlock the power of open source within a traditionally closed-off community. Now is the time for change.

What is free and open source software?

For decades, the tech industry has been greatly accelerated due to innovative free thinkers that fought for the freedom of studying, creating, and distributing software in the open. This is the core philosophy behind free and open source software (FOSS). This created an entire new perspective that enabled sharing and collaboration within a community, typically resulting in a faster time-to-market and greater adaptability.

Since then, many industries have followed suit, but the fashion industry's tendency to have a closed community has left it trailing behind. We want to change that.
