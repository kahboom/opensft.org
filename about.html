About

Open Source Fashion & Textile is an independent non-governmental body that exists to provide educational resources and tools based on free and open source technology to anyone within the fashion and textile industry.

An open source community fosters collaboration and makes it substantially easier for companies of varying sizes to pool together resources for important issues such as sustainability.

Who is behind OSFT?

OSFT primarily consists of volunteers with a passion to change the fashion industry for the better. Get to know us!
